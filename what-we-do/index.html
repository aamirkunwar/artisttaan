What We Do

More Than
A Label

ARTISTTAAN is India's first hip-hop label — and a full 360° management company. We find raw talent in the streets and build everything around it: the sound, the brand, the bookings, and the business, so artists can stay focused on the music.

Pillar One

The Label

Since 2016, we've been signing and developing India's most authentic hip-hop and indie voices — including the first Indian hip-hop deal with Sony Music.

A&R & Scouting

We go where the mainstream doesn't — into localities, cyphers, and street scenes — to find lyricists and producers with something real to say.

Music Production

Studio time, production credits, mixing and mastering — we help shape a raw idea into a record that's ready for release.

Distribution

Every release goes out across Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube and beyond, backed by playlist pitching and release-day strategy.

Publishing & Sync

Royalty collection, rights management, and placing our catalogue in films, shows, and ads so artists get paid for every use.

Pillar Two

360° Management

A record deal is the start, not the whole picture. Our management arm handles everything around the music, so an artist's career grows on every front at once.

Branding & Strategy

From visual identity to release calendars, we build the long-term story around every artist — not just the next single.

Brand Partnerships

We connect artists with brands for endorsements, collaborations, and campaigns that fit their voice and audience.

Live Shows & Bookings

College fests, concerts, and club nights — we handle routing, contracts, and logistics so artists can just perform.

PR & Media

Press coverage, interviews, and media relationships that keep artists visible between releases, not just around them.

Content & Video

Music videos, behind-the-scenes, and social content produced in-house to keep every drop visually on brand.

Merchandise

Direct-to-fan drops and merch lines that give artists another way to connect with — and earn from — their audience.
