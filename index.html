Our Story

Building India's Sound

Founded in 2016 by Aamir Kunwar, ARTISTTAAN began as a movement in the streets of East Delhi. What started as a passion project to support local rappers evolved into India's leading independent hip-hop and indie music label — and the first Indian hip-hop label to sign with Sony Music.

From a small locality in Jamnapaar, Aamir identified raw talent that mainstream labels wouldn't touch. Legends like Raga, Harjas, Yawar, and Muhfaad were among the first voices ARTISTTAAN amplified — artists who shaped the underground before Indian hip-hop had a mainstream.

That legacy lives on through today's roster — Shrey's poetic flows, Abir's soulful production, Slay's relentless energy, Apoorvv's indie heart, and Faraz's RnB precision. Every release carries the DNA of Indian streets, languages, and lived experiences.

We don't sign artists. We amplify voices that were already loud — just unheard.
