When people talk about the future of Uttarakhand's music scene, one name continues to stand out: GHILDIYAL.

Over the last few years, he has quietly built a reputation for being one of the most unique and versatile artists to emerge from the region. If anyone doubts that claim, all they need to do is listen to projects like *7 Mod paar* or tracks like *Tu Jaagi Reh*. From melodic records to hard-hitting rap cuts, GHILDIYAL has consistently shown an ability to move across genres and emotions without losing his identity.

His latest EP, *Puzzllle*, is perhaps the strongest example of that versatility yet.

Consisting of four tracks, the project showcases four completely different sides of the artist while maintaining a cohesive sound and vision throughout. Rather than staying in one lane, GHILDIYAL treats each song as a different piece of the puzzle, revealing another aspect of who he is as an artist.

The EP opens with *Kai Sani Ni*, featuring Kumaoni icon Tarun Bisht, widely known as "Mr. Kumaon." Produced by GHILDIYAL himself, the track immediately establishes the project's cultural roots while bringing together two artists from different generations of Uttarakhand's music movement. The visual experience adds another layer to the song, with the music video directed by Harsh Kandpal and Vishal SP Aesthetic. The result is a track that feels both modern and deeply connected to the hills.

The second track, *Tu*, is arguably one of the most addictive songs on the project. Built around a memorable hook that stays in your head long after the song ends, the record highlights GHILDIYAL's ability to create emotionally engaging music without sacrificing replay value. It's the kind of track that instantly finds a place in listeners' regular rotation.

Then comes *Insane*, featuring Onnu and produced by Baajewala. The track captures a different energy entirely. GHILDIYAL reflects on growth, identity, and the transition from where he started to where he stands today. The writing feels confident without being arrogant, and the record perfectly captures the mindset of an artist who understands his worth while continuing to push forward. Sonically and lyrically, *Insane* stands among the most powerful moments on the EP.

Closing the project is *M.A.D.*, a song that once again shifts gears and proves why GHILDIYAL cannot be boxed into a single category. Produced by TVZ and iiEE, the track brings a fresh perspective and energy, demonstrating yet another side of his artistry. By the time the EP reaches its final moments, it becomes clear that versatility isn't just a characteristic of GHILDIYAL's music—it's the foundation of it.

What makes *Puzzllle* special isn't simply the quality of its songs. It's the fact that every track feels different while still sounding unmistakably like GHILDIYAL. In an era where many artists find one formula and stick to it, he continues to experiment, evolve, and challenge expectations.

With *Puzzllle*, GHILDIYAL doesn't just deliver another EP. He reinforces his position as one of Uttarakhand's most exciting creative voices and shows exactly why he deserves to be part of the conversation whenever the state's most innovative artists are discussed.

If this project is any indication, GHILDIYAL is still only getting started.
